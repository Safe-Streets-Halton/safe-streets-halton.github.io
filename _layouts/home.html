Safer Streets for all in Halton Region

Safe Streets Halton is a community-run organization with volunteers across Halton Region. Our mission is to eliminate traffic deaths and improve the quality of life in Halton Region by enabling residents to safely and comfortably use sustainable and active modes of transportation.

We believe you should be able to travel safely, whether you walk, cycle, or drive.
