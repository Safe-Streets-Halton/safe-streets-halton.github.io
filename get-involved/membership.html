Annual Membership

Members are able to vote on the direction and strategies of the organization.

Membership Perks

  • A Safe Streets Halton Button
  • Voting on the Strategic Direction of the Organization
  • Elligibility for a seat on Committees or Board of Directors
  • And More to Come

Elligibility

Any person may become a member of Safe Streets Halton in accordance with our Membership Policy.
Only individuals 18 or older may become members.

Using the form below, you can obtain membership by either:

  • annually paying $10 or more; or
  • annually volunteering for 14 hours (or equivalent)
